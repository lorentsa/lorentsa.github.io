    About Meredith Grey

    My name is Meredith Grey. I am a character from the medical drama television series Grey's Anatomy.
    I am the Chief of General Surgery in Grey-Sloan Memorial Hospital in Seattle, Washington.
    In the series I struggle with the everyday life of being in a competitive profession, while having
    to deal with a dramatic love life and complex family. During my work in the hospital my colleagues
    became my family and helped me overcome the various tragedies in my life, which includes an airplane crash,
    drowning, the loss of my father Thatcher Grey, my beloved sister Lexie Grey, my brilliant mother Ellis Grey
    and the worst of them all – my spouse and life partner Derek Shepherd.
    I have three beautiful children which give me the power to get up in the morning despite the grief and sorrow.
    As the fifteenth season of my series is being aired my life gets more complicated, I engage in a new and
    exciting love affair, invent exciting new medical techniques and who knows what's more to come.
